NSU SubWings

HRD

The Human resource Development (HRD )of the NSU Campus Union enhances students' skills in arts, sports, and science through workshops, training programs, and access to resources. The RDB provides a supportive environment for students to develop their talents, pursue passions, and achieve their full potential.

Speaker's Forum

The Speakers Forum of the NSU Campus Union develops students' speech and presentation skills. We offer workshops, seminars, and practice sessions to enhance public speaking abilities. Our programs include guest lectures, debates, and competitions. Personalized coaching is also available.

Intelligentsia

The Intelligentsia Subdepartment of the NSU Campus Union cultivates students' general knowledge and political awareness. We provide engaging workshops and discussions on current affairs, global issues, and political trends.

Organizing Bureau

The Organizing Bureau of the NSU Campus Union hones students' skills in event management and social activities. We offer hands-on experience in organizing events, from planning to execution, and managing social functions

Media Wing

The Media Wing of the NSU Campus Union drives creativity in photography, videography, and graphic design. We offer hands-on workshops and projects to refine visual storytelling and design skills and also it's monitaring union social media activities.

English Wing

The English Wing of the NSU Campus Union elevates students' skills in writing, speaking, translation, and debate. We provide dynamic workshops and practice sessions to enhance your command of English and debating prowess.

Arabic Wing

The Arabic Wing of the NSU Campus Union fosters development in Arabic writing, speaking, and translation skills. We offer immersive workshops and practice sessions to enhance fluency and compositional expertise and arabic debate .

Urdu Wing

The Urdu Wing of the NSU Campus Union nurtures skills in Urdu writing, speaking, composition, and translation. We offer vibrant workshops and practice sessions to enhance your command of the language and debating flair.

Kannada Wing

The Kannada Wing of the NSU Campus Union enriches students' abilities in Kannada writing, speaking, composition, and translation. We offer engaging workshops and practice sessions to elevate your language skills and debating techniques.

Student Council

The Student Council of the NSU Campus Union is like mini campus union that champions the growth of junior students by coordinating with the Resource Development Board, Writers Forum, and various language wings. We facilitate skill development across diverse fields including arts, communication, and general knowledge.

Artotia

ARTORIA, the art club of the NSU Campus Union, ignites creativity in craft, design, drawing, and painting. We offer inspiring workshops and projects to enhance artistic skills and explore various mediums.

Science Club

The Science Club of the NSU Campus Union fuels innovation in scientific exploration and experimentation. This club fueled under HRD wing.That offer hands-on projects and discussions to enhance skills in research, technology, and practical science applications.

Welcome to the NSU Campus Union, where we are dedicated to enriching student life through a wide array of activities and programs.
